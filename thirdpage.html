Start of the Playoffs

The Boston Celtics started their historic playoff with the Miami Heat who eliminated them the year before but this time their best player Jimmy Butler was injured. The Celtics took a 1-0 lead after winning game 1 by 20 but in game 2 they lost at home. This evened the series 1-1 and from then on Celtics won three in a row by 20 plus points every game. Both Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown dominated this series. Then, next was the Confrence Semi Finals who was agains the Clevland Cavaliers. Once again they dropped one game at home and won the series pretty commandingly in 5 games. Donovan Mitchell and the Caviliers just did not have the same sort of star power the Celtics had.

Eastern Confrence Finals

The Eastern Confrence Finals was just as easy for the Celtics as the last two rounds were, they swept the Indiana Pacers in 4 games. Jaylen Brown won Eastern Confrence Finals MVP as he averaged 30 points and 5 rebounds. The Celtics once again over powered the Pacers with too much talent and star power. Derrick White and Jrue Holiday were exceptional as well as they perfected their roles. This was still without Kristaps Prozingis as he was still injured but making progress to getting back on the court for hopefully the NBA Finals.

Banner 18

Now the Boston Celtics were in the NBA Finals searching for Banner 18 or championship 18. They were matched up against the Dallas Maverics who had an excellent season and an even more impressive playoff run. They knocked out the Minnesota Timberwolves who had just beat the reigning champions the Denver Nuggets so the Mavericks were feeling great. Many reporters the Celtics would lose this matchup due to the star power of Luka Doncic and an old friend of the Celtics Kyrie Irving. Then there was also breaking news for the Boston Celtics it was that Porzingis was healthy and would return for the NBA Finals. He made an immediate impact in game 1 as he dominated the Mavericks making deep threes and blocking shots. The Celtics took a commanding 3-0 lead as they won the first three games without much of struggle. Then, the fourth game in Dallas the Mavericks took so the series was 3-1 and headed back to Boston for game 5. In Boston the Celtics won game 5 and won the NBA Finals giving them Banner 18! Jaylen Brown won Finals MVP as he averaged 21 points and 5 rebounds as well as played excellent defense on one of the best players in the NBA Luka Doncic.
